The Director

Sahil Bagga / Director Shabby

Director Shabby has built attention across Bollywood and Punjabi music by creating videos that feel premium, emotionally clear, and commercially sharp. His work pairs strong concepts with a practical approach to artist comfort, timelines, and budget.

He has worked with Mika Singh, Guru Randhawa, Millind Gaba, Tony Kakkar, Urvashi Rautela, Armaan Malik, B Praak, and many more, while delivering projects for labels and entertainment brands across the industry.

The portfolio includes recognition from CAMA Awards and Passion Vista, along with nomination visibility including PTC for standout releases such as Zindagi Di Paudi.
